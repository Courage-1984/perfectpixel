FAQ

Colorization Questions

How accurate are the colours?
Our AI model has been trained on hundreds of thousands of historical images and modern references. For portrait skin tones, our accuracy is extremely high. For clothing and backgrounds, we use historical research, and any colour information you provide is incorporated directly into the process for the most personal result.
Can I provide specific colour guidance?
Absolutely — and we encourage it. The more detail you can share (eye colour, hair colour, notable clothing colours, season/location details), the more personal and accurate your result will be. You can include written notes with your submission.
What types of photos can be colorized?
Any black and white photograph — portraits, group photos, wedding photos, military photos, historical scenes, architecture, and landscapes. Even severely damaged or low-resolution photos can be colorized, though we may recommend restoration as a first step.
Can I combine colorization with upscaling?
Yes — in fact, this is our most popular combination. We often upscale the photo first to recover detail, then apply colorization for the best possible result. Bundle pricing is available for combined services.
How many revisions are included?
Unlimited. We won't stop revising until the colours feel exactly right to you. This is your family's history — it needs to be perfect.
